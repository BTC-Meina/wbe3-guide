Bonus funds are usually used to redeem selected platform benefits, such as fee rebate vouchers, trading task rewards, or campaign rewards.

### Futures Trial Funds

Futures trial funds are usually used to experience futures trading. They may help new users learn opening positions, closing positions, margin, stop-loss, and take-profit settings.

However, futures trading is high risk. Trial funds may reduce initial practice cost, but they do not guarantee profit.

### Fee Rebate Vouchers

Fee rebate vouchers are used to reduce or refund trading fees for selected products. They may be more useful for active traders.

New users should not trade blindly just to complete tasks. Understand the rules first, then start with small amounts.
